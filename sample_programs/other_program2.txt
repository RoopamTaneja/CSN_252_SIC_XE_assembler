         COMP    #0

         COMP    #0
         JEQ     ENDINNR

         LDA     CHR
         STCH    STR,X

         STA     CHR

         LDX     IDX
         STCH    STR,X
         RMO     T,X

         JLT     INNER

         TIX     LEN
         JLT     SORT
